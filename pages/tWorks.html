Dome of the Rock

Medium: Chipboard, needle and thread, glue

Year: 2024

Project: Create an abstract respresentational building

This piece is my 3D abstract representational piece. My architectural theme was Islamic, so I recreated The Dome of the Rock. For my abstraction, I used a needle and thread to string together my pieces once they were laser cut, so my building could be pulled apart into its seperate layers.

Seasons Mobile

Medium: Chipboard, needle and thread, glue, paperclip, spray paint

Year: 2024

Project: Create an non-respresentational sculpture

This piece is my 3D non-representational piece. I was inspired by hanging baby mobiles and the four seasons, the middle swirling element represents time passing as the seasons rotate around the center.
