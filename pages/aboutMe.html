Autumn Kelley

Digital Works Traditional Works Videos and Animations

My name is Autumn Kelley. I am an out of state honors student from Georgia, majoring in Emerging Media (Pending) and minoring in Environmental Studies at UCF. I hope to go into the Animation and Visualization track and plan to graduate in May 2028.

I first got into animation in 2019 during middle school, when like many others, I decided I wanted to create an art Youtube channel. I watched and still enjoy creators such as Jaiden Animations, who were big inspirations. From learning on flipaclip on my phone, to taking a 2023 Pre-college program at Ringling and using industry standard programs, it has been a wonderful, growing art journey that I am excited to continue pursuing.

I love movement both within my art and physically. I specifically adore 2D animation, especially in older Disney movies such as Aristocats and of course, Studio Ghibli films. I believe the emphasis and beauty in the drawings help bring the stories to life, something I hope to do as an animator/artist as well. Everyone has a story, so being able to share and connect with others and convey complex thoughts and emotions is powerful. As for physical movement, I really enjoy running, weight lifting, biking, and rock climbing.
